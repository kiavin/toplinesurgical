    About

    About Us

    Topline Surgicals Limited is a trusted supplier of high-quality surgical and medical equipment in Kenya. Established in 2011, the company began as a small rehabilitation item distributor and has since grown into a leading one-stop shop for orthopedic, surgical, and general hospital supplies across the region. With a commitment to excellence, we serve a diverse clientele including major hospitals, government institutions, pharmacies, and top insurance companies.

    Our current clients include:

    • Mombasa Hospital.
    • Aga Khan Hospital.
    • Pandya Memorial

    Driven by our mission to provide affordable yet top-quality healthcare solutions, we source products through direct imports—ensuring value, reliability, and access to the latest medical innovations. We are proud to contribute to the healthcare system and support the country’s progress toward Vision 2030.
